Blue columns = Workwize gaps this case study addresses
Three gaps nobody has closed
Gap 1 · Partner data
Every player still uses email. Deel IT and Firstbase both have chain-of-custody records, but they're still dependent on partners manually reporting in. First mover to fix this wins the data layer.
Gap 2 · Slack
Slack is just a notification pipe everywhere. GroWrk lists it as an integration. Nobody has built approvals, inventory lookups, or employee self-service into Slack. That's a wide-open surface.
Gap 3 · Fleet visibility
No one has a real fleet dashboard. Every platform shows per-asset data as a flat list. None of them surface it as a Monday-morning regional health view with exportable reports for finance.
