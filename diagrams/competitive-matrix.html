Three gaps nobody has closed
Category gap 1
Partner data quality is broken everywhere. Every player, including Deel IT and Firstbase, still relies on email and manual updates from local partners. First mover wins this.
Category gap 2
Slack is just a notification channel. GroWrk lists it as an integration. Nobody has built approvals, inventory lookups, or self-service into Slack.
Category gap 3
No strategic fleet dashboard exists. Every platform has per-asset data. None surface it as a Monday-morning regional health view with exportable leadership reports.
