Case Study · Feature Prioritization
12 features I considered. 4 made the cut.
I started wide, looked at every gap the research surfaced, then filtered down using five criteria. The ones that didn't make it weren't bad ideas — they were wrong for this stage or wrong for Ilse.
12 considered
·
8 cut
·
4 selected
✓ Selected
3PL App
Strongest signal from reviews. No competitor has solved it. First mover wins the data layer.
✕ Cut
Refresh Planner
Lifecycle refresh modeling is valuable but overlaps with the Slack approvals layer as a decision-support surface.
✓ Selected
BambooHR Integration
Auto-onboarding is the core promise Workwize hasn't fully delivered. Real-time webhook closes the gap.
✕ Cut
Offboarding Risk Router
Started as a notification feature. Reframing helped, but it still felt thin next to the other four options.
✓ Selected
Slack Integration
Category-wide gap. Nobody has built real approvals and lookups in Slack. Wide-open surface with near-zero switching cost.
✕ Cut
Compliance/Audit Pack
Niche. Only relevant during active audit windows. Firstbase already covers the base version.
✓ Selected
Aggregated Dashboard
Every IT manager I found evidence of is still building CSV reports by hand. This is the Monday morning view they don't have.
✕ Cut
MDM Deepening
Real gap (Jamf/Intune pre-enrollment before ship), but the data foundation needs to exist first. Strong v2 candidate.
✕ Cut
Bulk Operations
Useful for M&A or layoff events, not daily use. Low frequency means smaller total impact.
✕ Cut
Equipment Policies Builder
Visual rule builder adds configuration burden. Most customers accept rougher policies in exchange for less setup work.
✕ Cut
Custom Reports Builder
Power-user feature that gets built and goes unused. The Aggregated Dashboard with CSV export covers 90% of the actual need.
✕ Cut
Employee Hub
Wrong framing. IT managers don't want employees managing their own equipment. Slack integration achieves the same goal more cleanly.
How I decided
01
Documented pain
Verified by real review quotes, not assumptions.
02
Serves Ilse directly
Not an end-employee feature. Her time, her KPIs.
03
Compounds with the others
Each feature builds on the one before it, not standalone.
04
Zero-touch alignment
Fits Workwize's stated mission. No features that add manual steps.
05
Shippable in 4 weeks
MVP scope fits inside the 3-month window without heroics.
