BambooHR Integration
One webhook, two scenarios. When a hire is created, equipment ships automatically. When someone leaves, retrieval and device wipe kick off the same way, no manual handoff required.

W
Workwize
Equipment orchestration
What happens on receive
1
Employee record created in Workwize with profile visible to Ilse.
2
Role mapped to equipment package using company-defined rules (AE gets MacBook Pro 14 + monitor + AirPods).
3
Location mapped to warehouse and local keyboard layout, shipping route calculated.
4
Start date sets delivery deadline, system works backwards to ensure arrival 3 days before day 1.
5
Shipment auto-initiated, Ilse gets one notification with 24-hour override window.
Net effect: new hire data becomes a ready-to-ship equipment order in Workwize within minutes. No manual data entry, no duplicate work between HR and IT.

W
Workwize
Equipment retrieval
What happens on receive
1
Employee status set to offboarding, assets flagged for retrieval in Workwize inventory.
2
Retrieval task created with return deadline calculated from last working day (typically last day + 5 business days).
3
Local 3PL partner notified to schedule a pickup at the employee's address, prepaid return label generated.
4
MDM wipe queued to trigger on last working day end-of-day, staged so the employee can finish their shift.
5
Ilse notified in Slack with a retrieval summary. One click to confirm or adjust the pickup window.
Net effect: termination in BambooHR triggers retrieval, device wipe, and partner coordination automatically. Ilse confirms once instead of coordinating five separate steps.
